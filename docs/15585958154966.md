## MPII 
MPII datasets has 16 keypoints
```
 (
 0 - r ankle, 1 - r knee, 2 - r hip, 
 3 - l hip, 4 - l knee, 5 - l ankle, 
 6 - pelvis, 7 - thorax, 8 - upper neck, 9 - head top, 
 10 - r wrist, 11 - r elbow, 12 - r shoulder, 
 13 - l shoulder, 14 - l elbow, 15 - l wrist
 )
```
